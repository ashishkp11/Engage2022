Car drivetrain Preference

Forward wheel drive (FWD) is the most sold vehicle drivetrain type. And the gap between FWD and rest of the drivetrain type is massive even though alot of SUVs are sold meaning people prefer FWD or they do not priorities having AWD,RWD or 4WD. For companies, not investing in drivetrains other than FWD could be beneficial as the rest of them are not sold as much.

Car Fuel type Preference

Even though going hybrid is the better option, stats show that people are not yet ready to shift to a different fuel type other than their traditional Diesel and petrol. Most of the car sold have either a petrol engine or a diesel engine with the later being the most sold one.

Car EMS Preference

Most cars sold have BS IV emmission rating which might not be the best and hence clearly shows that buyers do not look at this much while purchasing their vehicle. From a business point of few, just having BS IV rating in the vehicle should be enough for hitting proper sales numbers..

Transmission type Preference

Manual vehicles dominate the market. They are followed by automatic vehicles, which although have a very less sales number in camparision to the manual one, still put up a good numbers. Car manufacturers should avoid AMT and CVT type because they barely get sold.

Number of seats

Most of the cars sold were hatchbacks,sedans and SUVs so it is not a surprise to notice that most of the vehicle sold have 5-7 seats. Car companies that have famillies in focus sell the most cars. Hence cars with more seats hit the sales number far easier than any other car. For more sales focus, 5-7 seater cars should be the primary focus.
