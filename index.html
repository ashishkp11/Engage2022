About this Website

In this website, I have used dataset available on the Microsoft website to perform data analytics and find the best sales record of vehicles, the best category of vehicle, the best specs of a vehicle which results in most sales and the prominance on the safety feature. I have used PowerBI to perform the analytics and then the data was transfered to this website in the form of interactive. Scrolling over the graphs will present you with more information about certain aspects on the graph. The home page (below) consists of all the basic information of the type of vehicles sold. This gives the most in general information about the sales record of different vehicles. For other information such as Safety and Specs of vehicles, we can use the navigation bar (above) the give us other important information such as how important is the availability of airbags is to the buyer and how important is having "5 seats" are to buyers. All this information can help the manufacturer in producing a car that meets the market demands.

Car Body type Preference

The bar chart to the right shows the top 5 vehicle type sold in descening order. We can clearly see that SUV are the most sold vehicle followerd closely by hatchbacks and sedan. Crossovers and MPV (multi purpose vehicle) are the not actively prefered by people for purchase. There is not a staggering difference between the top 3 vehicle body types so the companies can priorities either of the three for maximum sales.
